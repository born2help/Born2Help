Participation Rules

Transparent Contribution

Every participant must act with clarity. All funds and actions are accountable to the mission.

Ethical Participation

Participation must never exploit, manipulate, or harm anyone. Humanity comes first.

Responsible Use

Tools, technology, and funds are used solely to reduce suffering and protect dignity.

Founder Safeguards

The Founder holds no ownership or financial privilege. Authority exists solely to safeguard purpose and prevent misuse.

Structural safeguards include financial locks, audit pathways, and mission-freeze mechanisms.

These rules ensure that the mission cannot be captured, misused, or altered by personal interests.
