How to Participate Safely

Our Philosophy

Every participant is part of a trust-based network. Contributions exist to relieve suffering, never to generate personal profit. Transparency, ethics, and founder safeguards ensure your participation has impact without risk of misuse.

Trust First

All contributions are allocated only for humanitarian relief and operational sustainability.

Founder Safeguards

The founder holds no personal financial privilege. Authority exists only to protect purpose and prevent misuse.

Transparency Rules

All allocations and use of funds are periodically disclosed for accountability.

Zero Interest Policy

No contribution is lent, grown, or circulated via interest (Riba). Ethics always come first.

Mission Lock

Structural safeguards prevent any single individual from changing mission, misusing funds, or bypassing accountability.
