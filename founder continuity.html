Founder Continuity & Succession Lock

Mission First — Not Power

B2H was established as an amanah (trust), not a transferable position of power. Leadership exists only to safeguard the mission — never to dominate it.

This ensures continuity, protection against capture, and immunity from greed-driven removal.

Founder Protection Clause

The founder cannot be removed or overridden as long as he remains:

  • Mentally capable
  • Ethically aligned with the Constitution
  • Actively safeguarding the mission

This prevents hostile takeovers, internal coups, popularity-based displacement, and capital-driven interference.

No Ownership — Only Guardianship

The founder does not own B2H. Authority is a responsibility to protect the people, not a personal right or privilege.

Limits on Founder Authority

The founder may NOT:

  • Redirect the mission for personal gain
  • Extract wealth or luxury
  • Suppress ethical accountability
  • Override the Constitution

Protection exists to guard purpose, not enable abuse.

Succession Through Trust — Not Ambition

Succession occurs only through private, lifetime designation by the founder. It is NOT automatic, public, or competitive.

Designated Custodians

Custodians are selected based on:

  • Proven character
  • Long-term restraint
  • Resistance to greed
  • Alignment with humanitarian purpose

No custodian gains ownership or personal entitlement.

Absolute Rejection of External Control

No external body — governments, investors, donors, committees, or boards — may remove the founder or override succession designations.

Emergency Continuity Clause

In case of sudden death or irreversible incapacity, authority immediately transfers to pre-designated custodians. No interim boards or temporary control bodies are allowed.

Protection Against Greed & Manipulation

B2H assumes some may attempt to seize authority with ambition or greed. Structural safeguards prevent anyone from climbing, negotiating, purchasing, or pressuring their way into power.

Authority is conditional. Purpose is permanent.

Final Ethical Anchor

Leadership continuity is governed by conscience, the Constitution, and responsibility before Allah — not by power, popularity, or capital.
