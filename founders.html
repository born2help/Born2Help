A Message from the Founder

Faith • Responsibility • Humanity

B2H was not created from wealth. It was created from responsibility — responsibility toward people, and responsibility before Allah.

Millions of families around the world struggle silently. Hunger, fear, and uncertainty exist behind closed doors, unseen by most, but deeply felt by those who live it.

Helping such people is not charity alone. It is an act loved by Allah and a duty placed upon every conscience.

B2H was born from one belief: that wealth should serve humanity, not control it.

When you participate in B2H, you are not only joining a project. You are directing real help, real dignity, and real relief to those who need it most.

⭐ THE FOUNDER’S FINAL WILL

A Message to Every Future Leader of B2H — Preserved Forever

  1. Protect the Poor Before Protecting the Treasury: Wealth is secondary. Humanity comes first.
  2. Put Truth Before Popularity: Always choose what is right, even if it is unpopular.
  3. Hold the Mission Tighter Than Power: B2H belongs to the world, not to leaders.
  4. Never Let Greed Destroy What Love Built: Reject promises, shortcuts, or money that harm the mission.
  5. Protect the Liquidity Like It Is Sacred: Trust is everything — preserve it.
  6. Egos Kill Movements — Humility Builds Empires: Lead with kindness, patience, and humility.
  7. Every Holder Is Family: Respect all participants regardless of wealth.
  8. Continue Expanding: Grow B2H responsibly — the world needs it.
  9. When You Feel Weak, Read These Words: Remember why B2H exists — hope, humanity, and purpose.
  10. If One Day You Fall: Rise again. Seek counsel. Stand firm. Protect the mission above all.

— Founder, B2H
